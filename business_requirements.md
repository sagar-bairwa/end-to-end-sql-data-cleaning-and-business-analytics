# 📋 Business Requirements

This document outlines the business questions explored during the SQL E-Commerce Analysis project.

---

# 📊 Data Understanding

Understanding the structure and quality of the data before analysis.

- Total rows in each table
- City-wise customer distribution
- Products per category
- Most expensive products
- Earliest and latest order date

---

# 📈 Basic Insights

Key business metrics generated from the dataset.

- Total Revenue
- Orders per Customer
- Top 5 Customers by Number of Orders
- Top 5 Most Sold Products
- Category-wise Revenue

---

# 💼 Business Analysis

Business-focused questions to understand customer behavior and sales performance.

- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Repeat Customers
- Customers with No Orders
- Average Order Value per Customer
- Total Amount per Order

---

# 🚀 Advanced Analysis

Advanced SQL analysis using Window Functions and CTEs.

- Top 3 Customers per City (by Revenue)
- Top-selling Product per Category
- Running Total Revenue
- First & Last Order per Customer
- Customers Ordering the Same Product Multiple Times

---

# 🎯 Final Business Insights

High-level findings useful for decision-making.

- Highest Revenue Generating City
- Most Profitable Category
- Top 5 High-value Customers
- Inactive Customers
- Month with Highest Revenue Growth
